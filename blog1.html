Behind the Scenes of Wizeperience Festival Campaign

The Wizeperience Festival was one of the most exciting projects I worked on as a Social Media Specialist. The goal was to create a brand experience that resonated with Gen-Z and Millennials through social media channels. Hereâ€™s how I approached it:

Understanding the Audience

Knowing your audience is key. For this project, the primary audience was music-loving young adults who value creativity and engagement. I crafted content that spoke their language, using vibrant visuals and interactive social media challenges.

Execution and Strategy

We utilized a mix of paid ads and organic posts to build hype. The campaign was structured around the release of the lineup, ticket sales, and day-of-event coverage. Each phase had specific goals, from brand awareness to driving ticket sales.

Results

The campaign generated a significant buzz on social media, with engagement rates exceeding 40% on key posts. Ticket sales were 20% above expectations, thanks to the high engagement and strategic use of ads.
